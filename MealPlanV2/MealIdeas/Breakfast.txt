Title:
Eggs and Bacon
Ingredients:
Eggs
Bacon
Avocado
Garlic Salt
Paprika
SeasonAll
Toast
Recipe:
Cook up the bacon and save some of the grease once the bacon is done.
Season the avocado with the spices to taste. Toast the toast.
Cook your eggs your preferred way.
Once the eggs and toast are done, put the avocado on the toast and top with your egg!
